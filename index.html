Music

One of my major hobbies is music and everything related to it. I listen to it almost all the time while doing something, and in my free time I like playing guitar and sometimes record it. Miniatures

Another big hobby of mine is tabletop wargaming and especially its collecting aspect. I like building, converting (sometimes sculpting) and painting miniatures, partially due to collection building and also because I feel for me it's a fine line between art and craftsmanship which lets me truelly relax.
